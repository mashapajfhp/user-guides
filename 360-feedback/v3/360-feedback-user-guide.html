What is 360° Feedback?

Overview

360° Feedback is Bayzat's comprehensive performance review tool that enables organizations to collect multi-dimensional feedback on employee performance. Unlike traditional top-down reviews, 360° Feedback gathers insights from multiple perspectives including peers, managers, direct reports, and self-assessments, providing a holistic view of an employee's strengths, development areas, and overall contribution to the organization.

The feature supports creating customized surveys with flexible question types and rating scales, allowing organizations to tailor the feedback process to their specific performance management needs. Surveys can be created using AI-assisted methods or manual configuration, with support for up to 4 participant groups per review cycle.

Key Benefits

  • Multi-Perspective Insights: Collect feedback from various stakeholder groups to eliminate bias and gain comprehensive performance understanding
  • Customizable Survey Design: Create tailored surveys with AI assistance or manual configuration, supporting various question types and rating scales (3-10 points)
  • Flexible Participant Groups: Structure reviews with up to 4 distinct participant groups (peers, managers, direct reports, self)
  • Structured Performance Assessment: Use standardized rating scales with custom labels and descriptions for consistent evaluation
  • Preview and Validation: Review and validate surveys before launching to ensure quality and clarity

Who Uses This Feature

  • HR Administrators: Configure rating scales, create survey templates, manage review cycles, and oversee the feedback collection process
  • Performance Managers: Initiate 360° reviews for team members, select participant groups, and customize survey questions
  • Employees: Participate in 360° reviews by providing feedback on colleagues and completing self-assessments
  • Line Managers: Review aggregated feedback results and use insights for performance discussions and development planning
Business Value: 360° Feedback transforms performance management from a one-dimensional process into a comprehensive development tool. Organizations using multi-rater feedback report improved self-awareness, more targeted development plans, and stronger alignment between individual performance and organizational goals.

Initial Setup

Before creating your first 360° feedback survey, complete these foundational configuration steps to ensure consistent and meaningful evaluations across your organization.

Step 1: Configure Rating Scale

The rating scale is a critical foundation for 360° feedback surveys. Set this up before creating any surveys.

  1. Navigate to Settings → Performance → Rating Scale
  2. Click Edit Rating Scale
  3. Select the number of rating points (3-10 points)
    • Default is 5-point scale
    • Consider your organization's evaluation culture when choosing
  4. Decide whether to include a "Not Applicable" (N/A) option
    • Recommended for competencies that may not apply to all roles
  5. Customize labels for each rating point
    • Example for 5-point scale: Needs Improvement, Below Expectations, Meets Expectations, Exceeds Expectations, Outstanding
  6. Add descriptions for each rating point to ensure consistent interpretation
    • Descriptions help reviewers understand what each rating level represents
  7. Save your rating scale configuration
Best Practice: Use clear, objective language in your rating scale labels and descriptions. Avoid ambiguous terms that could be interpreted differently by various reviewers.

Step 2: Define Competencies and Review Criteria

Identify the competencies and behaviors you want to assess through 360° feedback:

  • Leadership skills
  • Communication effectiveness
  • Teamwork and collaboration
  • Technical expertise
  • Problem-solving abilities
  • Adaptability and innovation

Document these competencies as they will guide your survey question creation.

Step 3: Identify Participant Groups

Determine which stakeholder groups will provide feedback in your 360° reviews:

Participant GroupPurposeTypical Number
SelfEmployee's self-assessment1 (the employee)
ManagerDirect supervisor's perspective1-2
PeersColleague feedback at same level3-5
Direct ReportsSubordinate perspectives (for managers)3-5

You can select up to 4 participant groups per review cycle.

Step 4: Plan Survey Structure

Before creating your first survey, plan the structure:

  1. Question Categories: Group questions by competency area
  2. Question Types: Decide on mix of rating scales, open-ended questions, and multiple choice
  3. Survey Length: Balance comprehensiveness with completion time (aim for 10-15 minutes)
  4. Question Wording: Ensure questions are clear, specific, and behavior-focused

Step 5: Verify System Requirements

Confirm your organization meets these requirements:

  • ✓ Performance module is enabled in your Bayzat plan
  • ✓ 360° Feedback feature is activated
  • ✓ Admin users have appropriate permissions
  • ✓ Employee data is up-to-date (reporting relationships, departments)
  • ✓ Rating scale is configured

Troubleshooting & Edge Cases

Troubleshooting & Edge Cases

This section addresses common issues you may encounter when working with 360° Reviews and provides solutions for edge cases.

Survey Creation Issues

Cannot Save Survey

Symptom: Survey save button is disabled or survey fails to save.

Possible Causes:

  • Required fields are not completed
  • Participant groups not selected (minimum 1, maximum 4 groups required)
  • No questions added to the survey
  • Invalid question format or missing question text

Solution:

  1. Verify all required fields are completed
  2. Ensure at least one participant group is selected
  3. Add at least one question to the survey
  4. Review question formatting and ensure all questions have text
  5. Use the preview function to validate survey structure
Participant Group Selection Errors

Symptom: Unable to select desired participant groups or groups appear unavailable.

Solution:

  • Verify that participant groups have been configured in Settings
  • Check that you have not exceeded the 4-group maximum per review
  • Ensure selected employees exist in the system and are active
  • Confirm you have appropriate permissions to assign participants

Survey Management Edge Cases

Need to Modify Survey After Creation

Issue: Survey content needs to be changed after creation, but editing is not supported.

Impact: This is a known limitation (JRV-1937). Surveys cannot be edited once created.

Workaround:

  1. Create a new survey with the corrected content
  2. If the original survey has not been sent to participants, delete it and use the new version
  3. If responses have been collected, note the limitation in your feedback analysis
  4. For future surveys, implement a thorough review process before creation
⚠️ Important: Always preview surveys thoroughly before finalizing. Once created, survey content cannot be modified. Plan for a review cycle with stakeholders before launching surveys to employees.
Missing or Incomplete Responses

Symptom: Expected feedback responses are not appearing or are incomplete.

Possible Causes:

  • Participants have not completed the survey
  • Survey deadline has not passed
  • Email notifications were not received
  • Participants lack access to the platform

Solution:

  1. Check survey status and response completion rates
  2. Verify participant email addresses are correct
  3. Send reminder notifications to non-respondents
  4. Confirm participants have active Bayzat accounts and appropriate access
  5. Extend survey deadline if necessary

Reporting and Analytics Limitations

Cannot Generate Consolidated Reports

Issue: Need to view feedback across all participants but consolidated reporting is not available.

Impact: This is a known limitation (JRV-1932, JRV-1933, JRV-1938, JRV-1939). The platform does not support consolidated report viewing for all participants or dedicated views for line managers and employees.

Workaround:

  • Export individual feedback reports and compile manually
  • Use spreadsheet tools to aggregate data
  • Create custom reports using exported data
  • Schedule regular review meetings to discuss feedback trends
Limited Analytics Capabilities

Issue: Need advanced analytics features like trend analysis, comparative reports, or data visualization.

Impact: This is a known limitation (JRV-1912). The platform lacks dedicated reporting and analytics features for 360 feedback.

Workaround:

  • Export raw data for analysis in external tools (Excel, Power BI, Tableau)
  • Create custom dashboards using exported data
  • Maintain historical data tracking outside the platform
  • Use third-party analytics tools for deeper insights

Rating Scale Configuration Issues

Rating Scale Changes Not Reflected

Symptom: Modified rating scale does not appear in active surveys.

Solution:

  • Rating scale changes apply to new surveys only
  • Existing surveys retain the rating scale active at creation time
  • Create new surveys to use updated rating scales
  • Note: Navigate to Settings > Performance > Rating Scale to modify scales

Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions
What is 360° feedback?
360° feedback is a comprehensive performance review process where employees receive confidential, anonymous feedback from multiple sources including managers, peers, direct reports, and sometimes external stakeholders. This multi-perspective approach provides a holistic view of an employee's performance and behaviors.
Who can create 360° Review surveys?
Typically, HR administrators and performance management leads with appropriate permissions can create and manage 360° Review surveys. Contact your Bayzat administrator to verify your access level.
How many participant groups can I include in a survey?
You can select up to 4 participant groups per 360° Review survey. Groups may include managers, peers, direct reports, and other relevant stakeholders.
Can I use AI to help create surveys?
Yes, the platform offers AI-assisted survey creation alongside manual creation methods. This can help streamline the survey development process and suggest relevant questions.
Survey Creation & Management
Can I edit a survey after creating it?
No. This is a known limitation (JRV-1937). Once a survey is created, its content cannot be edited. Always preview and review surveys thoroughly before finalizing. If changes are needed, you must create a new survey.
What question types are available?
The platform supports various question types for 360° feedback surveys. Specific types include rating scales, open-ended text responses, and multiple-choice questions. Check the question customization options when creating your survey.
How do I preview a survey before sending it?
Use the preview functionality available during survey creation. This allows you to see exactly how the survey will appear to participants before launching it.
Can I save a survey as a draft?
Survey draft functionality depends on your platform version. Use the save option during creation to preserve your work before finalizing.
Rating Scales
How do I customize rating scales?
Navigate to Settings > Performance > Rating Scale. You can select between 3-10 rating points, choose to include a "Not Applicable" (N/A) option, and customize labels and descriptions for each point.
Do rating scale changes affect existing surveys?
No. Rating scale changes apply only to new surveys created after the modification. Existing surveys retain the rating scale that was active when they were created.
What is the default rating scale?
The default rating scale is 5 points. You can customize this to any value between 3 and 10 points based on your organization's needs.
Reporting & Analytics
Can I view consolidated reports for all participants?
No. This is a known limitation (JRV-1932, JRV-1933, JRV-1938, JRV-1939). The platform does not currently support consolidated report viewing for all participants or dedicated views for line managers and employees. You will need to compile individual reports manually or export data for external analysis.
Are advanced analytics available?
No. This is a known limitation (JRV-1912). The platform lacks dedicated reporting and analytics features for 360 feedback. For advanced analysis, export data and use external tools like Excel, Power BI, or Tableau.
Can I export feedback data?
Check your platform's export functionality for available data export options. This can help you create custom reports and perform external analysis.
How do I track response rates?
Monitor survey status and response completion through the survey management interface. This helps you identify non-respondents and send reminders as needed.
Participant Management
How do participants receive survey notifications?
Participants receive email notifications when assigned to a 360° Review survey. Ensure participant email addresses are correct and that they have active Bayzat accounts.
What if a participant doesn't receive the survey?
Verify the participant's email address is correct, check that they have an active Bayzat account with appropriate access, and consider sending a manual reminder or resending the notification.
Can participants see who provided feedback?
360° feedback is designed to be confidential and anonymous to encourage honest responses. Participants should not be able to identify individual respondents.
