Feature Overview

What is Attendance?

The Attendance module is a comprehensive time and attendance tracking system that enables organizations to monitor employee work hours, manage shift schedules, and maintain accurate attendance records. The system supports both mobile app-based check-ins and biometric device integrations, providing flexibility for different workforce types and operational requirements.

Who Uses This Feature?

    • Employees: Check in and out of work, record breaks, request attendance regularization, and view their attendance history
    • Line Managers: Monitor team attendance, approve regularization requests, edit attendance records for direct reports, and manage shift assignments
    • Shift Schedulers: Create and manage weekly shift schedules, assign employees to work centers, and adjust shift timings
    • HR Administrators/Super Admins: Configure work timings, set up offices and work centers, manage attendance policies, generate reports, and oversee system-wide attendance settings
    • Time Off Managers: Review attendance data in relation to leave requests, manage kiosk mode for bulk attendance marking
    • People Managers: Access attendance reports and manage company-wide attendance configurations

    Key Capabilities

    • Mobile App Check-In/Out: Employees can mark attendance using the Bayzat mobile app with geo-fencing, location verification, and optional image capture
    • Biometric Integration: Support for biometric devices via API, CAMS, or external vendor integrations for physical attendance tracking
    • Shift Scheduling: Static shifts for routine operations and dynamic shift scheduler for fluctuating business demands with weekly schedule views
    • Work Timings Configuration: Define working hours, late arrival thresholds, early departure rules, break times, and overtime calculation methods
    • Attendance Regularization: Employee-initiated ticket system for correcting check-in/check-out times with configurable approval workflows
    • Multiple Visits/Geo-Fencing: Track employee visits to multiple locations with configurable radius settings and client assignments
    • Comprehensive Reporting: Daily reports, custom reports, location reports with downloadable data and edit functionality
    • Time & Pay Adjustments: Automatic tracking of late arrivals, early departures, absences, and extra hours for payroll integration
    • Kiosk Mode: Bulk attendance marking for employees without smartphones or email access

    Business Value

    The Attendance module delivers significant operational efficiency by automating time tracking, reducing manual attendance management overhead, and providing accurate data for payroll processing. Organizations benefit from:

    • Elimination of buddy punching through geo-fencing and biometric verification
    • Real-time visibility into workforce presence and scheduling
    • Reduced administrative burden through automated notifications and reminders
    • Accurate overtime and extra hours calculation for fair compensation
    • Flexible scheduling capabilities to match business operational patterns
    • Comprehensive audit trails for attendance modifications and policy changes
    • Integration with payroll systems for seamless compensation processing

Product Foundation

Technical Prerequisites

System Requirements

    • Mobile App: Bayzat mobile app installed on iOS or Android devices for app-based attendance tracking
    • Location Services: Device location services must be enabled for geo-fencing and location verification
    • Biometric Devices (Optional): Compatible biometric hardware for physical attendance tracking, configured with serial numbers and office assignments
    • Network Connectivity: Stable internet connection for real-time attendance synchronization

    Dependencies on Other Modules

    • Employee Records: Employee profiles must exist in the HR system before attendance tracking can be configured. The Attendance module pulls employee names, IDs, and reporting structures from the Employee Records page
    • Leave Management: Integrates with the Leave/Time Off module to automatically handle attendance records when leave requests are approved. When leave is approved, existing attendance records for the leave period are deleted
    • Payroll: Time & Pay Adjustments feed into payroll processing. Attendance records marked as "Approved for Payroll" are used for compensation calculations
    • Requests/Ticketing System: Attendance Regularization uses the Custom Tickets framework for employee-initiated attendance corrections
    • Approval Flows: Attendance Regularization and Time & Pay Adjustments require configured approval workflows with assigned roles

    Licensing and Plan Requirements

    The Attendance module is available as part of the Bayzat HR platform. Specific features may have different availability:

    • Basic Attendance Tracking: Available on standard HR plans
    • Shift Scheduler: Requires attendance module activation
    • Biometric Integration: May require additional configuration and third-party vendor setup (CAMS, API integration, or external vendors)
    • Advanced Features: Facial recognition, multiple visits tracking, and attendance API settings may have specific plan requirements

    Contact your Bayzat account representative to confirm feature availability for your organization's plan.

    Integration Points

    • Bayzat Mobile App: Primary interface for employee check-in/check-out with real-time synchronization
    • Biometric Devices: Integration via API, CAMS portal, or external vendor systems for physical attendance tracking
    • Admin Panel Audit Logs: Tracks all attendance record modifications, work timing changes, and policy assignments (90-day retention)
    • Looker Reports: Advanced reporting and analytics for attendance data, including fraud detection dashboards
    • Time & Pay Adjustments: Automatic creation of deduction/addition records based on attendance policy violations

    User Roles and Permissions

    The following roles have access to Attendance features:

    • Super Admin: Full access to all attendance settings, reports, and configurations
    • Time Off Manager: Manage attendance settings, view reports, enable image capture, use kiosk mode
    • People Manager: View attendance reports, manage some company configurations (restricted from office location editing)
    • Line Manager: View and edit attendance for direct reports, approve regularization requests, manage team attendance
    • Shift Scheduler: Create and manage shift schedules for assigned work centers
    • Attendance Manager: Edit office locations, manage attendance configurations
    • Employee: Check in/out, view personal attendance records, submit regularization requests
