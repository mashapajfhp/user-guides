What is Attendance Management?

Overview

The Attendance module is a comprehensive time and attendance tracking system that enables organizations to monitor employee work hours, manage shift schedules, and maintain accurate attendance records. The system supports mobile app-based check-ins, biometric device integrations, and geo-fencing for location verification.

Key Benefits

  • Real-time check-in/check-out tracking via mobile app with geo-fencing
  • Flexible shift scheduling with static and dynamic shift support
  • Automated notifications 10 minutes before check-in/out times
  • Comprehensive attendance reports with daily, custom, and location views
  • Attendance regularization workflow for correcting missed entries

Who Uses This Feature?

  • Employees: Check in/out via mobile app, view attendance history, request attendance regularization
  • Line Managers: Monitor team attendance, approve regularization requests, edit attendance records
  • Shift Schedulers: Create and manage weekly shift schedules, assign employees to work centers
  • HR Administrators: Configure work timings, set up geo-fencing, manage attendance policies
  • Time Off Managers: Review attendance data, manage overlapping requests

Product Foundation Overview

Core Concepts

Work Timings

Define working hours, late arrival thresholds, early departure rules, break times, and overtime calculation methods.

Work Centers

Physical locations where employees work. Each work center can have its own shift scheduler and assigned employees.

Shift Scheduler

Weekly view for assigning employees to shifts. Supports both static (fixed) and dynamic (variable) shift patterns.

Geo-fencing

Location-based verification ensuring employees check in from authorized locations with configurable radius.

Biometric Integration

Physical attendance tracking using fingerprint scanners or other biometric devices connected via API or CAMS integration.

Facial Recognition

Identity verification during check-in using the mobile app camera, preventing buddy punching and ensuring accurate attendance.

Setup Process

Configuring Attendance Settings

Before employees can use attendance tracking, administrators must configure the core settings.

1

Configure General Attendance Settings

Navigate to Settings → Attendance → General Settings. Configure check-in/out methods including mobile app, facial recognition, and biometric options.

2

Set Up Work Timings

Go to Settings → Attendance → Work Timings. Create timing schedules defining start/end times, break duration, and overtime thresholds.

3

Configure Geo-fencing (Multiple Visits)

Navigate to Settings → Attendance → Multiple Visits. Enable location tracking and set the check-in radius (default 100m).

4

Create Work Centers

Go to Settings → Attendance → Work Centers. Add locations with schedulers, work timings, offices, and employee assignments.

Tip: Ensure office locations have accurate GPS coordinates for geo-fencing to work correctly. Test check-in from the actual location before rolling out to employees.

Editing Attendance Records (Managers)

  1. Navigate to Time → Employee Attendance
  2. Locate the employee's attendance record in the report
  3. Click the Edit button in the row
  4. Modify check-in/check-out times as needed
  5. Save changes

Note: Attendance records are locked to the work timing configuration at check-in. Changes to work timings will not retroactively affect historical records.

Business Rules & Limitations

Core Business Rules

Rule Description Impact
Shift publication required Shifts must be published as working days before check-ins are accepted Early or late check-ins on unpublished days are rejected
Location validation Geo-fencing validates employee location against office coordinates Check-ins outside radius may be flagged or rejected
Leave approval impact Approved leave requests delete existing attendance records Historical attendance data is not preserved when leave is approved
Configuration lock Attendance records locked to work timing at check-in Cannot retroactively update records after configuration changes

System Constraints

  • Employees cannot record attendance for half-day leave periods
  • No bulk status change functionality for attendance adjustments
  • Approved extra hours automatically add to leave balance without reversal option
  • Custom roles require explicit policy assignment to appear in approval flows

Known Limitations

Half-Day Leave: Employees cannot record attendance and HR cannot view or edit attendance for half-day leave periods. This is a current system limitation.

Attendance Variance Reporting: Cannot export or display pre-edit attendance values in reports, limiting tracking of attendance modifications.

Overtime Validation: Hours worked are calculated automatically without validating checkout legitimacy or indicating missing/manipulated records.

Edge Cases

  • Multiple location visits: Enable Multiple Visits feature for employees who work at multiple client sites daily
  • Biometric vs App conflicts: When both methods are used, ensure device serial numbers are correctly mapped to offices
  • Timezone considerations: Attendance times are recorded in the configured office timezone

Support Resources

Frequently Asked Questions

Why is my check-in being rejected?

Check-ins are rejected if the shift is not published, you're outside the geo-fence radius, or you're checking in outside the allowed time threshold. Contact your manager or HR for assistance.

How do I correct a missed check-in or check-out?

Submit an Attendance Regularization request through Requests → My Tickets → Create Ticket → Select "Attendance Regularization". Your manager will review and approve the correction.

Can I check in from home or a remote location?

This depends on your company's geo-fencing policy. If remote work is allowed, your HR administrator may configure specific locations or disable geo-fencing for your profile.

Why don't I see my overtime hours?

Overtime tracking requires you to be assigned to an overtime policy. Contact your HR administrator to verify your policy assignment.

How do notifications work?

The system sends push notifications 10 minutes before your scheduled check-in and check-out times. Ensure notifications are enabled for the Bayzat app on your device.
