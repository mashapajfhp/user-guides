What is Knowledge Hub?

Overview

Knowledge Hub is Bayzat's centralized knowledge management platform that transforms how organizations store, organize, and access company information. It replaces scattered documents and inefficient information retrieval with an AI-powered, hierarchical content system.

Key Benefits

  • AI-Powered Search: Find information instantly using natural language queries with Bayzat AI chatbot
  • Organized Content Structure: Create Spaces, Folders, and Pages with granular permission controls
  • Seamless Collaboration: Share knowledge via direct links or Newsfeed with role-based access
  • Smart Activity Tracking: Monitor content updates and engagement with real-time activity feeds

Setup Process

Creating Your First Space

Spaces are the top-level containers for organizing content. Follow these steps to create and configure your first Space.

1

Navigate to Knowledge Hub

Click Company in the main navigation, then select Knowledge Hub from the dropdown menu.

2

Click Create

Click the Create button in the top-right corner and select 'Create new space' from the dropdown.

3

Enter Space Details

Provide a descriptive Name (e.g., "HR Policies") and Description explaining the Space's purpose.

4

Set Initial Permissions

Choose who can access the Space: 'Only me and admins', 'All employees can view', or 'Custom' for granular control.

5

Add Content

Within your Space, create folders to organize topics, add pages with rich text content, and upload supporting files.

Tip: Start with 'Only me and admins' permission while building content, then change to 'All employees can view' once ready to share.

Managing Permissions

Access permissions are configured at the Space level and automatically inherit to all folders and pages within.

Permission Levels

Level Description Use Case
Only me and admins Only creator and admins can view Draft content, confidential documents
All employees can view Any employee can view; editors can modify Company policies, general resources
Custom Granular control per user Targeted content for specific users, teams, roles, or groups

Individual Permission Types

Permission Capabilities
Editor Can create, edit, and delete content within the Space
View only Can view content but cannot make changes
No access Cannot see or access the Space

Sharing Content

Share Spaces, folders, and pages using multiple methods:

  1. Copy Link: Generate a shareable URL that can be sent via email or chat
  2. Publish on Newsfeed: Share directly to company Newsfeed with mobile notifications
  3. Share with People: Add specific users with custom permission levels

Note: Super Admins and Knowledge Hub Managers automatically have editor access to all company Spaces. Their permission level cannot be changed.

Support Resources

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find information in Knowledge Hub?

Use the Bayzat AI chatbot for natural language search, browse Spaces directly, or check your Favorites and Recent files on the Overview page.

Can I share content with external users?

No, Knowledge Hub content can only be shared with registered employees on your Bayzat platform. Use Copy Link for internal sharing.

How do I recover deleted content?

Access the Trash from Knowledge Hub menu. Deleted items remain for 30 days and can be restored. After 30 days, content is permanently deleted.

Why can't I edit a Space?

You need Editor permission for that Space. Contact the Space owner or a Knowledge Hub Manager to request edit access.
