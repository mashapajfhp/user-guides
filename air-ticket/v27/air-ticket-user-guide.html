Feature Overview

What is Air Ticket?

Air Ticket is a comprehensive feature within Bayzat that enables organizations to manage employee air ticket allowances efficiently. It provides a flexible system for creating customizable policies, tracking requests, and offering multiple redemption options‚Äîwhether through reimbursement, company-booked tickets, or encashment of allowances.

Air Ticket simplifies the management of employee travel allowances by enabling organizations to create and manage air ticket policies with customizable eligibility criteria, allowance amounts, and redemption options. Employees can request air tickets through the Bayzat mobile app using three different methods: reimbursement for tickets they've already purchased, ticket booking where HR/Admin handles the purchase, or encashment of their air ticket allowance.

Key Benefits

Organizations gain transparent control over travel allowance spending with customizable policies tailored to different employee levels and regions. Employees enjoy flexibility in how they use their allowances, with streamlined request processes through the mobile app. The feature reduces administrative overhead through automated workflows and clear approval processes.

How It Works

HR administrators create air ticket policies specifying allowance amounts, eligible employees, and redemption options. Employees then submit requests through the mobile app, selecting their preferred redemption method. Requests flow through approval workflows and can be automatically processed using configurable automation rules.

Product Foundation

Core Concepts

Air Ticket operates on three fundamental concepts: policies (organizational rules defining allowances and eligibility), requests (employee submissions using available allowances), and redemption methods (how employees receive their benefits). Policies can be customized by employee seniority, location, or organizational hierarchy, and each employee can be assigned to only one air ticket policy.

Business Context

Air ticket allowances are a key employee benefit, particularly in the UAE and KSA regions where international travel is common. Organizations need to manage these allowances transparently while controlling costs and ensuring compliance with company policies. Air Ticket provides the framework for this balance.

Related Features

Air Ticket integrates with the Bayzat mobile app's Work section for employee requests, Bayzat Payroll for encashment processing, Finance menu for admin management, and Workflows for automation. Approval processes are managed through existing air ticket approval workflows.

Prerequisites

Before implementing Air Ticket in your organization, ensure you have the necessary access, data, and system configurations in place.

Access Requirements

To set up air ticket policies, you must have HR administrator or manager access to the Bayzat platform with permissions to navigate Settings > Company > Air ticket policies. Employees need access to the Bayzat mobile app with the Work section enabled. The air ticket request options must be enabled by the admin before employees can submit requests.

Data Requirements

Organizations must have employee records in the system with defined seniority levels or organizational hierarchy if policies will vary by these criteria. For ticket booking requests, employees must be able to share relevant travel details with the HR team. For encashment requests, the system must have access to employee leave records to verify eligibility.

System Configuration

Bayzat Payroll must be configured if you plan to use encashment as a redemption option. Approval workflows must be set up for air ticket requests. The mobile app must be deployed to employees with the Work section accessible. For automated encashment workflows, the Workflows feature must be enabled and configured.

Troubleshooting

This section addresses common issues you may encounter when using the Air Ticket feature, along with practical solutions and prevention strategies.

Unable to Request Air Ticket

Symptoms:

  • Request Air Ticket button is not visible or clickable
  • Error message appears when attempting to submit request
  • Mobile app crashes when accessing Air Ticket section

Cause: Air ticket request options may not be enabled by your administrator, or you may not have the required permissions.

Solution: Contact your HR administrator to verify that air ticket request options are enabled in your organization's settings. Ensure your user account has the necessary permissions to submit air ticket requests.

Prevention: Administrators should enable air ticket request options during initial setup and verify permissions are correctly assigned to all eligible employees.

Encashment Request Rejected

Symptoms:

  • Encashment option is greyed out or unavailable
  • Error message: 'Cannot process encashment at this time'
  • Encashment request is automatically declined

Cause: Encashment is only possible if the employee is not taking any leaves. If an employee has active or pending leave requests, encashment cannot be processed.

Solution: Ensure all leave requests are completed or cancelled before attempting to request encashment. Check your leave calendar to confirm no overlapping leave periods exist.

Prevention: Plan encashment requests during periods when no leaves are scheduled. Coordinate with your HR team to ensure leave and encashment requests do not overlap.

Cannot Edit Active Policy

Symptoms:

  • Edit button is disabled for policy
  • Error message: 'Cannot modify active policy with existing requests'
  • Policy changes do not save

Cause: Active policies cannot be edited once ticket requests are raised. This prevents inconsistencies in policy terms after employees have already submitted requests.

Solution: Create a new policy with the desired changes and assign it to employees going forward. Archive or deactivate the old policy if no longer needed.

Prevention: Finalize all policy details before activating and communicating to employees. Plan policy changes during renewal cycles to minimize disruption.

Request Exceeds Allowance Amount

Symptoms:

  • Error: 'Request amount exceeds allocated allowance'
  • Cannot submit request for desired ticket amount
  • System prevents booking or reimbursement

Cause: Employees cannot request tickets exceeding their allotted allowance. The system enforces allowance limits to control costs.

Solution: Reduce the ticket amount to match available allowance, or request partial reimbursement/encashment. Contact HR to discuss allowance adjustments if needed.

Prevention: Review your available allowance before submitting requests. Plan ticket bookings within allocated limits.

Workflow Encashment Not Triggering

Symptoms:

  • Scheduled encashment workflow did not execute
  • No encashment request created at scheduled time
  • Workflow shows as active but no activity log entries

Cause: Workflow trigger time may be incorrectly configured, or the workflow may not have been properly saved. Time selection is based on UAE or KSA account location.

Solution: Verify workflow configuration: check trigger date/time, ensure correct time zone is selected, and confirm the workflow is in active status. Re-save the workflow if needed.

Prevention: Test workflows in a non-production environment first. Verify trigger times account for your organization's time zone. Monitor workflow activity logs regularly.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the three ways employees can request air tickets?

Employees can request air tickets through three methods: (1) Reimbursement - submit receipts for reimbursement of ticket costs, (2) Ticket Booking - request HR/Admin to book tickets on their behalf, or (3) Encashment - receive cash equivalent of their air ticket allowance.

Can an employee be assigned to multiple air ticket policies?

No, employees cannot be assigned to multiple air ticket policies. Each employee can only have one active air ticket policy. If you need to change an employee's policy, remove them from the current policy before assigning them to a new one.

What happens if an employee's ticket request exceeds their allowance?

The system will not allow employees to request tickets exceeding their allotted allowance. Employees must either reduce the ticket amount to match their available allowance or contact HR to discuss allowance adjustments.

When can employees request encashment of their air ticket allowance?

Encashment is only possible if the employee is not taking any leaves. Employees should ensure they have no active or pending leave requests before submitting an encashment request.

Can I edit an air ticket policy after employees have submitted requests?

No, active policies cannot be edited once ticket requests are raised. To make changes to policy terms, you must create a new policy with the desired modifications and assign it to employees going forward.

How do I set up automated encashment requests?

Use the Air Tickets Auto Encashment workflow feature in Settings > Workflows. Create a new workflow, select the Air Tickets Auto Encashment recipe, define your trigger time and criteria, and activate the workflow. Encashment requests will be automatically generated based on your specified schedule.
