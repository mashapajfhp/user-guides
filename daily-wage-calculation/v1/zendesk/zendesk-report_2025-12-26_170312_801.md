## Feature Overview

Daily wage calculation is a payroll feature that automatically deducts unpaid leave from an employee's salary by computing a daily pay rate. The daily pay is calculated by dividing the total salary (basic plus allowance) by the number of actual working days in the month. Public holidays are subtracted from the working days count to arrive at the actual working days used in the calculation. The feature supports both calendar days (e.g., 30 days) and working days (e.g., 22 days) leave policies. Upon approval of an unpaid leave request, an automatic pay deduction is created and reflected on the payroll table.
