What is Records Management?

Overview

Records Management centralizes all employee information, documents, and lifecycle events in one platform. Access it from Company > All Employees or navigate directly to employee profiles.

View, edit, and manage employee data across 12 profile tabs including personal info, work details, documents, payroll, and attendance. Search, filter, and bulk-action capabilities streamline record management.

Key Benefits

  • Eliminate manual spreadsheet updates with real-time profile completion tracking
  • Automate document expiry reminders to prevent compliance gaps
  • Centralize 11 configurable document types with OCR-powered data extraction
  • Track employee lifecycle from hire to offboarding in one system
  • Enable self-service employee access via platform invitations
  • Generate org charts automatically from reporting relationships

Product Foundation Overview

How Records Management Fits

Records Management is the transactional layer where employee data lives throughout the employment lifecycle. Employee records are individual profiles containing personal, work, document, payroll, and compliance information.

Mental model: Employee Record (profile) ‚Üí Contains all employee data ‚Üí Referenced by all modules ‚Üí Drives payroll, attendance, leave, insurance, and compliance

One employee record affects multiple downstream processes. Changes to work information, documents, or personal data immediately impact payroll calculations, leave balances, attendance tracking, and insurance eligibility.

Key Decisions Before Setup

Answer these questions before creating employee records:

  • What document types are mandatory for compliance? ‚Äî Configure employee document types with expiry tracking before onboarding
  • Which fields require approval workflows? ‚Äî Decide if employees can edit personal/work info or if changes require admin approval
  • How will you structure reporting hierarchies? ‚Äî Define departments, offices, and reporting managers to enable org chart generation
  • What custom data points do you need to track? ‚Äî Identify additional employee attributes beyond standard fields (custom employee data)

Related Features

  • Payroll Module ‚Äî Pulls employee salary, bank details, and work information for monthly payroll processing and end of service calculations
  • Time Off ‚Äî Uses hire date, work profile, and leave cycle type to calculate leave balances and encashments
  • Attendance ‚Äî References work timings, office locations, and employee status for check-in/check-out tracking
  • Health Insurance ‚Äî Links employee records to insurance policies, tracks dependent documents, and manages endorsements
  • Document Management ‚Äî Stores and tracks employee documents with expiry reminders sent to admins and managers

Initial Setup

First-Time Configuration

Before adding employees, configure document types and office locations to ensure complete record management.

Step 1: Configure Employee Document Types

Navigate to Settings ‚Üí Company ‚Üí Employee Document Types to define mandatory and optional documents.

  • Review 11 default document types (Passport, Emirates ID, Residency Visa, Others)
  • Click 'Add new' to create custom document types
  • Set document name (2-70 characters, supports Arabic)
  • Mark as mandatory or non-mandatory
  • Enable expiry date tracking (toggle ON/OFF)
  • Configure email reminders (1-12 months before expiry)

Note: Default document types cannot be deleted. The 'Others' document type has expiry toggle locked to TRUE but expiry date entry is not mandatory.

Step 2: Set Up Office Locations

Navigate to Settings ‚Üí Company ‚Üí Offices to create office locations for employee assignment.

  • Click 'Add New' in Offices section
  • Enter office name and location (auto-populates geo-location)
  • Optionally adjust attendance check-in radius
  • Save office location

Step 3: Configure Company Bank Details

Navigate to Settings ‚Üí Payroll ‚Üí Bank Accounts to store bank details for payroll processing.

  • Click 'Add New' in Bank Accounts section
  • Enter bank account details
  • Select associated Trade License (if multiple exist)
  • Save account details

Step 4: Upload Company Logo

Navigate to Settings ‚Üí Company ‚Üí Company Logo to add your brand to the platform and payslips.

  • Click 'Change logo'
  • Upload logo file
  • Save changes

Result: Logo appears on platform interface, employee payslips, and end-of-service settlements.

Task: Calculate End of Service Settlement

Calculate final payment for departing employees including gratuity, leave encashment, and deductions.

Subtask: Access End of Service Calculator

Navigate to Employee Profile > Payroll tab > End of Service sub-tab. Service Information section displays Hire Date, Basic Salary for departure month, Departure Date picker, Total Service Duration, Reason for departure dropdown, Contract Type dropdown, and Calculate button.

Subtask: Configure Departure Details

  • Select Departure Date using date picker
  • Choose Reason for departure: Resignation with notice, Termination with/without notice, Resignation without notice, End of contract, Death, Absconding

Subtask: Review Settlement Calculation

System auto-calculates gratuity per MOL formula based on service duration and contract type. Review leave encashments pulled from time-off data. Check monthly pay proration. Add optional additions or one-off deductions. Review outstanding loans and recurring deductions. Verify final settlement amount.

Subtask: Submit EOS Transaction

Add reference numbers and notes if needed. Click Submit to process End of Service transaction. Print final settlement document for employee records. Employee removed from payroll when offboarded and no unpaid amounts remain.

Subtask: Send Bulk Invitations

Select multiple employees using checkboxes. 'Send Invite N' button appears. Click to open bulk invite dialog. Enter work email for each selected employee. Click Invite to send all invitations.

Navigate to Company > Invitations > Invite Employees. Enter First Name, Last Name, Nationality, Work Email per row. Click 'Invite another colleague' to add more rows. Add optional custom message. Click Invite to send all invitations.

Approval Flow

Overview

Records Management supports approval workflows for employee data changes, document modifications, and profile updates through the Approval flows configuration.

Available Approval Categories

The platform provides 13 approval flow categories. Relevant to Records Management:

  • Employee Form Onboarding ‚Äî Controls approval for new employee record creation
  • Leave ‚Äî Manages leave request approvals tied to employee records
  • Expense ‚Äî Approves work expense submissions from employee profiles
  • Payroll Transaction ‚Äî Controls approval for payroll changes affecting employee records

Configuration Structure

Each approval flow category has two configuration paths:

  • Advanced (If conditions) ‚Äî Set conditional approval rules based on employee attributes, amounts, or other criteria
  • Default (Else) ‚Äî Define fallback approval path when conditions aren't met

Approval Flow Setup

Document Change Approvals

Document Edit Restrictions: Based on PRD evidence, employee document editing can be configured to require approval. When enabled, employees must request changes instead of directly editing documents, personal info, work info, or dependent data.

Approval workflow triggers email notifications to designated approvers. Admins can approve or reject document modification requests.

The Leave flow shows Super Admin as default approver in step 1. This pattern applies to other employee record-related approvals where management review is required before changes take effect.

Business Rules & Limitations

Business Rules

Employee Record Management

  • Legal Names Required: Use legal names as they appear in official documents for all employee records.
  • Work Email Mandatory: Valid work email required for platform access. Personal email can be used in work email field if official email unavailable.
  • Employee Status: Mark employees as inactive with departure date and reason rather than deleting records to preserve history.
  • Compliance Information: Accurate identification numbers (trade license, freezone IDs, Emirates ID) required to prevent legal issues.
  • Hire Date Restrictions: Hire date changes impact leave balances, time-off requests, and payroll calculations across modules.

Document Management

  • Document Name Length: Document type names must be 2-70 characters, supports Arabic.
  • Expiry Reminders: Maximum 12 months before expiry. Default is 7 months for Passport, Residency Visa, Emirates ID.
  • "Others" Document Type: Expiry date toggle locked to TRUE but expiry date entry not mandatory. Mandatory status cannot be changed (remains non-mandatory).
  • Emirates ID: Both front and back have same fields. Quick action available to copy details between front/back.
  • File Size Limit: Maximum 5MB per document upload.
  • Accepted Formats: gif, bmp, heic, jpeg, jpg, jp2, svg, tiff, tif, webp, png, pdf.
  • Bulk Upload Limit: Maximum 20 documents per upload batch.

End of Service Calculation

  • Gratuity Rules: Calculated per UAE labor law. Non-AED employees receive 0 gratuity.
  • Contract Types: Different handling for limited and unlimited contracts affects gratuity eligibility.
  • Departure Date: Submit EOS only when departure date is past or current month. Variable pay and work expenses not available for future months.
  • Salary Configuration: Not editable once departure date is set.
  • Rehire Restriction: Rehire only available if EOS not submitted.

Employee Import/Export

  • Regional Date Formatting: Turkish region settings may cause unexpected date format outputs in Excel exports.
  • Hidden Columns: Excel imports fail when rows contain hidden ID columns. Do not copy-paste rows with hidden system IDs.
  • Type Column Protection: Cannot change "employee or dependant" column for existing records during import.
  • Validation Errors: System validates residency visa location against country of residence. UAE-specific fields only shown when Legal Country of Residence is UAE.

FAQs & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I bulk import employee records via Excel?

Yes, use Add Employee > Excel bulk import. Download the template, fill employee data, and upload. The system validates data on import and reports errors (e.g., invalid residency visa location when country is not UAE). Pre-existing data inconsistencies may block re-import.

Why can't I change an employee's work email after they've registered?

Once an employee registers on Bayzat, the work email field is locked to prevent account conflicts. Contact Customer Support via the chat feature to request email changes for registered users.

What happens when I delete an employee profile?

Deletion is permanent and removes all related information including documents, insurance plans, and payroll records. The system shows a generic confirmation dialog regardless of how much data exists. Consider using offboarding instead to preserve records while removing access.

Why doesn't OCR extract data from my uploaded documents?

OCR may misclassify document types (e.g., passport detected as Emirates ID) and fail to extract data. Manually select the correct document type from the dropdown and enter data in the Fill details form. OCR works best with clear, high-resolution images of supported document types.

Can I edit insurance-linked documents?

No. Documents linked to insurance policies (Insurance Card Front/Back) are view-only with no Edit or Actions buttons. Regular employee documents (Passport, Other) have full Edit and Actions options.

Why does the Send Invite dialog close without sending invitations?

The dialog lacks frontend email validation. If you click Invite without entering work emails, the system triggers silent server errors and closes the dialog with no visible feedback. Always enter valid work emails before clicking Invite.

Can I change an employee's hire date after it's set?

Yes, but the UI shows a misleading tooltip stating "Hire date can not be changed due to selected individual leave cycle type." The field is actually editable and saves successfully. Be aware this may affect leave balances, time-off requests, and payroll calculations.

How do I handle document expiry reminders?

Configure expiry reminders (1-12 months before expiry) in Settings > Company > Employee Document Types. The system sends automated email reminders to Super Admins, People Managers, and document owners. Default reminder is 7 months for Passport, Residency Visa, and Emirates ID.

What if I need to delete an employee and their manager at the same time?

The system cannot process concurrent deletions of employees with reporting relationships due to transaction conflicts. Delete the reporting employee first, then delete the manager, or remove the reporting relationship before deletion.

Why can't I see all employees with missing documents when I filter?

The "Missing information" filter only shows employees with incomplete profiles (isCompleted=false), not all employees missing specific document types. Use the Employee Documents page to view missing documents across all employees.

Can employees with inactive status be mentioned in comments?

Currently yes, but this creates confusion. The system does not restrict mentions of offboarded or inactive employees in leave requests or attendance comments. Verify employee status before mentioning them.

Why does the employee list export show incorrect date formats?

Regional settings (e.g., Turkish locale) may cause date formatting issues in Excel exports. The platform has localization constraints that affect date rendering in some regional configurations.
