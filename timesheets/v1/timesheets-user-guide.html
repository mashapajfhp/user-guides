What is Timesheets?

Overview

Timesheets captures employee work hours and attendance data for payroll processing. Access it through the Time & Attendance module to review, approve, and manage time entries submitted by employees.

Review submitted timesheets, approve or reject time entries, and export data for payroll integration.

  • Eliminate manual timesheet collection and data entry errors
  • Standardize time tracking across all departments and locations
  • Automate approval workflows for faster payroll processing
  • Ensure compliance with labor regulations and company policies
  • Generate audit trails for all time and pay adjustments

Initial Setup

Prerequisites

Before using Timesheets, ensure these configurations are complete:

  • Work Timings schedules configured with start/end times
  • Attendance policies enabled for relevant employee groups
  • Line manager assignments completed in employee profiles
  • Overtime calculation rules defined (if applicable)

Access Configuration

Grant appropriate permissions to users who will manage timesheets:

  1. Navigate to Settings ‚Üí Users & Permissions
  2. Assign "Time & Attendance" module access to line managers
  3. Enable "Approve Time Adjustments" permission for approvers
  4. Configure notification preferences for pending requests

Employee Onboarding

Communicate timesheet submission requirements to employees:

  • Timesheet submission deadlines (typically weekly or bi-weekly)
  • How to request time adjustments for missed clock-ins/outs
  • Overtime request approval process and timelines
  • Where to view timesheet status and approval history

Note: Timesheet entries are automatically generated based on attendance clock-in/out data. Manual timesheet creation by admins is not currently supported.

FAQs & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I find the bulk approval option for overtime requests?

Bulk approval for extra hours is located in the Time and Pay Adjustment section, not the main timesheet view. Line managers have the same bulk approval capabilities as super admins.

Can I delete multiple adjustment requests at once?

No, the UI does not support bulk deletion. Contact support for backend processing if you need to delete multiple requests.

How do I handle shifts that span midnight?

Create two separate timesheet entries: one ending at 23:59 on the first day and another starting at 00:00 on the second day. The system cannot process time entries spanning multiple days.

Can I edit an employee's timesheet as an admin?

No, admins cannot directly create or modify employee timesheets. Request the employee to make corrections or contact support for backend adjustments.

How do I filter adjustment requests by line manager?

Line manager filtering is not currently available. Use search functionality or manual review, or contact support for specific reporting needs.

Why can't I see detailed action logs for timesheet changes?

Action logs and timestamps may be difficult to locate due to limited filtering options. Contact support if you need specific historical data.
