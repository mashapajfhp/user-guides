## Glossary

- **Working Days**: Standard calculation of 26 working days per month, derived from 365 annual days minus 52 weekly offs, resulting in 313 working days per year divided by 12 months [Tickets: TSSD-4905, TSSD-1807]
- **Daily Wage Calculator**: A system configuration area in payroll settings that determines how daily wage is calculated, particularly for unpaid leave scenarios. [Tickets: TSSD-2648]
- **Greyed-out Formula**: A formula display state in leave policy indicating that the current configuration is being overridden by payroll settings and cannot be directly edited. [Tickets: TSSD-2648]
- **Daily Pay Calculation**: Calculating daily wage by dividing total monthly salary by 30.41 (average days in a month) [Tickets: TSSD-1581]
- **End of Service (EOS) Gratuity**: A financial benefit calculated based on an employee's total service period, typically computed using daily wage and years of service, with specific calculation rules for different service duration brackets [Tickets: TSSD-4179, TSSD-1625]
- **Pro-rated Amount**: A proportional salary calculation for partial periods, typically computed by dividing total salary by standard days (30) and multiplying by actual days worked [Tickets: TSSD-2605]
- **Service Duration**: Total number of working days an employee has worked, calculated by counting days from start date to departure date, including potential special considerations like leap year days [Tickets: TSSD-1625]
- **Daily Wage Calculation**: Method of computing an employee's daily rate, which in this platform uses a fixed divisor of 30 days for basic salary, differing from some clients' preferred (Basic × 12 / 365) approach. [Tickets: TSSD-2043]
- **Gratuity Calculation**: End of service benefit calculation that considers an employee's length of service and daily wage, with the platform using a fixed 21 days per year for service between 1-5 years. [Tickets: TSSD-2043]
- **Daily Wage Proration**: Calculation of daily wage by dividing total monthly salary by a specific number of days (e.g., 30 or 30.42 days). [Tickets: TSSD-2321]
- **Daily Wage Divisor**: A calculation method that divides monthly salary by 30.42 days instead of calendar days when computing leave deductions [Tickets: TSSD-4368]
- **Unpaid Leave Deduction**: Calculation of salary reduction for days an employee is absent without pay, based on dividing monthly salary by actual calendar days in the month [Tickets: TSSD-4368, TSSD-1301, TSSD-1807]
- **Global Daily Rate Configuration**: A system-wide setting that determines how daily rates are calculated for unpaid leave, which can override individual policy configurations [Tickets: TSSD-1753]
- **Basic Salary**: The base salary component used in daily wage calculations, distinct from gross salary [Tickets: TSSD-1753]
