## Glossary

- **Working Days**: A standard of 26 working days per month, calculated as 365 (annual days) - 52 (weekly offs) = 313 total working days, divided by 12 months to get 26.08 working days per month. [Tickets: TSSD-4905, TSSD-3648, TSSD-4292, TSSD-4464, TSSD-1753, TSSD-1807]
- **Daily Wage Calculator**: The calculated per-day monetary value used in gratuity and end-of-service benefit calculations, typically derived by dividing monthly basic salary by a fixed number of days. [Tickets: TSSD-2648, TSSD-2043, TSSD-4464]
- **Unpaid Leave Deduction**: A calculation method where an employee's salary is reduced proportionally for days not worked, based on the total calendar days in a month. [Tickets: TSSD-2648, TSSD-1301, TSSD-1807]
- **End of Service (EOS) Gratuity**: End of service benefit calculated based on employee's length of service, daily wage, and contract type, typically involving a formula of daily wage multiplied by days per year and total service years [Tickets: TSSD-4179, TSSD-2605, TSSD-4742, TSSD-1625, TSSD-2252]
- **Leap Year Handling**: Special calculation method for partial years that includes a leap year, using 366 days as the divisor instead of 365 to account for the extra day. [Tickets: TSSD-4179, TSSD-2252]
- **Pro-rated Amount**: A proportional salary calculation for an employee leaving mid-month, computed by dividing total salary by 30 and multiplying by days worked [Tickets: TSSD-2605]
- **Monthly Accrual Policy**: Leave policy where leave days are earned and granted at the end of each month, not prorated for incomplete months. [Tickets: TSSD-4742]
- **Conditional Accrual Policy**: Leave policy where leave days are earned daily instead of being granted at month-end, allowing more flexible prorating. [Tickets: TSSD-4742]
- **Service Duration**: Total number of working days an employee has worked, calculated by counting days from start date to departure date, including considerations for leap years [Tickets: TSSD-1625]
- **Gratuity Calculation**: The process of computing an employee's end-of-service benefit based on salary, length of service, and applicable calculation rules. [Tickets: TSSD-2043]
- **Daily Rate Divisor**: The number of days used to calculate the daily wage in leave salary calculations, configured in the leave policy and not automatically adjusted based on month length [Tickets: TSSD-4731]
- **Leave Salary Accrual**: Cumulative calculation of vacation leave monetary value that varies based on working days configuration [Tickets: TSSD-3648]
- **Daily Wage Proration**: The method of calculating a proportional daily wage based on the total monthly salary, typically by dividing the monthly salary by a standard number of days (e.g., 30 or 30.42). [Tickets: TSSD-2321, TSSD-4292]
- **Prorated Salary**: A partial salary calculation based on the exact number of days worked in a given month, typically calculated by dividing monthly salary by total calendar days and multiplying by days actually worked. [Tickets: TSSD-3058]
- **Daily Wage Divisor**: A system-configured value of 30.42 used to calculate daily wage by dividing monthly salary, instead of using exact calendar days. [Tickets: TSSD-4368]
- **Calendar Days**: The total number of days in a specific month, which can vary between 28-31 days depending on the month and whether it's a leap year. [Tickets: TSSD-1301]
- **Basic Salary**: The base component of an employee's salary used for calculating various deductions and benefits, distinct from gross salary [Tickets: TSSD-1753]
