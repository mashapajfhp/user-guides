## Glossary

- **Daily Wage**: Calculated by dividing monthly salary by a specific number of days (typically 30, 30.42, or working days), used for prorating salary, leave calculations, and end of service benefits. [Tickets: TSSD-2321]
- **Working Days**: Days considered for salary calculation, which may exclude weekends and public holidays based on the specific work week configuration. [Tickets: TSSD-4292]
