## Glossary

- **Working Days**: Days configured as working in an employee's work week configuration, which may include all 7 days of the week depending on the specific work week policy. [Tickets: TSSD-4905, TSSD-4292, TSSD-1807]
- **Pro-rated Amount**: Proportional salary calculation for partial month based on basic salary plus allowances divided by 30 days [Tickets: TSSD-2605]
- **End of Service (EOS)**: Final salary settlement for an employee leaving the company, calculated based on their total service period [Tickets: TSSD-2605]
- **Gratuity**: End of service benefit calculated based on employee's length of service, typically computed by multiplying daily wage by a factor depending on years of service [Tickets: TSSD-1625, TSSD-2043]
- **Service Days**: Total number of days worked, including departure date, calculated by counting actual calendar days and dividing by 365 to determine service years [Tickets: TSSD-1625]
- **Daily Wage Proration**: Calculating salary proportionally based on the number of working days in a given period, determined by the employee's configured work week. [Tickets: TSSD-2043, TSSD-2321, TSSD-4292]
- **Daily Rate Divisor**: A configurable setting in the leave policy that determines how daily wage is calculated, typically set to a fixed number of days (e.g., 30 or 31) used to prorate salary for leave periods. [Tickets: TSSD-4731]
- **Prorated Salary**: Partial salary calculation based on the exact number of days worked in a month, typically used when an employee starts or leaves mid-month [Tickets: TSSD-3058]
- **Daily Wage Divisor**: A system configuration that divides monthly salary to calculate daily wage, using 30.42 days instead of calendar days for leave and payroll calculations. [Tickets: TSSD-4368]
- **Unpaid Leave Deduction**: Calculation of salary reduction based on number of days an employee is absent without pay, using daily rate divided by actual calendar days in the month. [Tickets: TSSD-4368, TSSD-1301, TSSD-1807]
- **Daily Rate Calculation**: Method of calculating daily wage by dividing salary (basic or gross) by total working days [Tickets: TSSD-1753]
