## Glossary

- **Working Days**: A standard of 26 working days per month used as the basis for salary calculation, derived from 365 annual days minus 52 weekly offs, resulting in 313 working days per year. [Tickets: TSSD-4905, TSSD-3648, TSSD-4292, TSSD-4464, TSSD-1807]
- **Daily Wage Calculator**: The calculated per-day monetary value derived from an employee's basic monthly salary, used in gratuity and end-of-service benefit calculations. [Tickets: TSSD-2648, TSSD-2043, TSSD-4464]
- **Unpaid Leave Deduction**: Calculation of salary reduction for days an employee takes leave without pay, typically computed as (total_salary/30.41) * number of unpaid leave days [Tickets: TSSD-2648, TSSD-1581, TSSD-2532, TSSD-4368, TSSD-1301, TSSD-1753]
- **End of Service (EOS) Gratuity**: A financial benefit calculated based on an employee's total service period, typically computed using daily wage and years of service, with specific calculation rules for different service duration brackets. [Tickets: TSSD-4179, TSSD-2605, TSSD-4742, TSSD-1625, TSSD-2252]
- **Leap Year**: A year containing 366 days instead of the standard 365 days, occurring every 4 years (with some exceptions), which can impact calculations involving days and service periods. [Tickets: TSSD-4179]
- **Pro-rated Amount**: A proportional salary calculation for partial month periods, typically calculated by dividing total salary by 30 and multiplying by days worked [Tickets: TSSD-2605]
- **Monthly Accrual Policy**: Leave days are earned and credited at the end of each month, not prorated for partial months [Tickets: TSSD-4742]
- **Conditional Accrual Policy**: Leave days are earned daily instead of being credited at month-end, allowing more flexible prorating [Tickets: TSSD-4742]
- **Service Duration**: Total number of days worked, calculated by counting from start date to departure date, including potential leap year considerations [Tickets: TSSD-1625]
- **Gratuity Calculation**: The process of computing end-of-service benefits for an employee based on their salary, length of service, and applicable labor regulations. [Tickets: TSSD-2043]
- **Daily Rate Divisor**: The number of days used to divide monthly salary to calculate a daily wage rate, which can be configured in leave policy settings (e.g., 30 or 31 calendar days). [Tickets: TSSD-4731, TSSD-1807]
- **Leave Salary Accrual**: Cumulative calculation of vacation leave monetary value, which can vary based on the number of working days in a given month [Tickets: TSSD-3648]
- **Daily Wage Proration**: Calculation of salary based on the number of days worked, typically using a divisor to determine the daily rate (e.g., Basic salary + allowances / 30.42 days). [Tickets: TSSD-2321, TSSD-4292]
- **Leave Salary Calculation**: Prorating an employee's salary during periods of leave, which may use a different calculation method from regular salary proration. [Tickets: TSSD-2321]
- **Prorated Salary**: A partial salary calculation based on the exact number of days worked, typically used when an employee joins or leaves mid-month [Tickets: TSSD-3058]
- **Daily Wage Divisor**: A system-configured value (30.42 days) used to calculate daily wage rates for payroll deductions, which differs from standard calendar days. [Tickets: TSSD-4368]
- **Calendar Days**: Total number of days in a specific month, which can vary (e.g., 29 days in February during a leap year) [Tickets: TSSD-1301]
- **Global Daily Rate Configuration**: A system-wide setting that determines how daily wage deductions are calculated, which takes precedence over individual policy configurations [Tickets: TSSD-1753]
