## Glossary

- **Daily Pay Calculation**: Calculated by dividing total monthly salary by 30.41 to determine daily wage rate [Tickets: TSSD-1581]
- **Pro-rated Amount**: A proportional salary calculation for partial periods, typically calculated by dividing total salary by 30 days and multiplying by days worked [Tickets: TSSD-2605]
- **Daily Wage Proration**: Calculated by dividing basic monthly salary by a fixed 30-day divisor, representing the per-day compensation value used in gratuity and leave calculations [Tickets: TSSD-2043, TSSD-2321, TSSD-4464]
- **Gratuity Calculation**: End of service benefit computation based on years worked, daily wage rate, and service duration, using a fixed formula of 21 days per year for service between 1-5 years [Tickets: TSSD-2043]
- **Leave Salary Calculation**: Method of calculating salary for days an employee is on leave, typically prorated based on total monthly salary and days of leave. [Tickets: TSSD-2321]
- **Working Days**: Days counted for salary proration, which can be configured differently per work week and may include all 7 days of the week [Tickets: TSSD-4292, TSSD-4464]
- **Daily Wage Divisor**: A fixed number (30.42) used to calculate the daily wage by dividing monthly salary, used in unpaid leave deduction calculations instead of using exact calendar days [Tickets: TSSD-4368]
- **Unpaid Leave Deduction**: A calculation method where daily wage is prorated based on the number of calendar days in a month, with deductions applied for days not worked. [Tickets: TSSD-1301]
- **Calendar Days**: The total number of days in a specific month, which can vary (e.g., 28/29 days in February, 30 or 31 days in other months). [Tickets: TSSD-1301]
