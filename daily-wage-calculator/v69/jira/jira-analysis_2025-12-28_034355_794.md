## Glossary

- **Working Days**: A standard of 26 working days per month used as the basis for salary calculation, derived from 365 total days minus 52 weekly offs, resulting in 313 working days per year. [Tickets: TSSD-4905, TSSD-3648, TSSD-4292, TSSD-4464, TSSD-1807]
- **Daily Wage Calculator**: A system configuration that determines how daily wage is calculated, typically by dividing salary components by number of days [Tickets: TSSD-2648, TSSD-1625, TSSD-2252, TSSD-2043, TSSD-4464]
- **Unpaid Leave Deduction**: Calculation of salary reduction based on the number of calendar days an employee is absent without pay, using the daily wage rate. [Tickets: TSSD-2648, TSSD-2532, TSSD-1301, TSSD-1753, TSSD-1807]
- **Daily Pay Calculation**: Calculated by dividing total monthly salary by 30.41 to determine daily wage rate [Tickets: TSSD-1581]
- **End of Service (EOS) Gratuity**: Termination benefit calculated based on employee's service duration, daily wage, and tiered rates for different service periods. [Tickets: TSSD-4179, TSSD-2605, TSSD-4742, TSSD-2252]
- **Leap Year**: A year with 366 days, occurring every 4 years, which requires special handling in date-based calculations [Tickets: TSSD-4179]
- **Pro-rated Amount**: A proportional salary calculation for an employee who works only part of a standard pay period, typically calculated by dividing total salary by days in the month. [Tickets: TSSD-2605]
- **Monthly Accrual Policy**: Leave days are earned and granted at the end of each month, not prorated for incomplete months. [Tickets: TSSD-4742]
- **Conditional Accrual Policy**: Leave days are earned daily instead of being granted at month-end, allowing more flexible accrual tracking. [Tickets: TSSD-4742]
- **Service Duration**: Total number of days worked, calculated by counting from start date to departure date, including potential leap year considerations. [Tickets: TSSD-1625]
- **Gratuity Calculation**: End of service benefit calculation that uses daily wage, years of service, and a per-year rate (21 days in this example). [Tickets: TSSD-2043]
- **Daily Rate Divisor**: The number of days used to divide monthly salary when calculating daily wage, which can be configured in leave policy settings [Tickets: TSSD-4731]
- **Leave Salary Calculation**: Process of determining compensation for periods of employee leave, based on daily rate and policy-configured divisor [Tickets: TSSD-4731]
- **Leave Salary Accrual**: Cumulative calculation of vacation leave monetary value, which can vary based on monthly working days configuration [Tickets: TSSD-3648]
- **Daily Wage Proration**: Calculation of salary or leave compensation based on a daily rate, typically derived by dividing monthly salary by a specific number of days (e.g., 30 or 30.42). [Tickets: TSSD-2321, TSSD-4292]
- **Prorated Salary**: Partial salary calculation based on actual days worked, typically calculated by dividing monthly salary by total calendar days and multiplying by days worked [Tickets: TSSD-3058]
- **Daily Wage Divisor**: A calculation method that divides monthly salary by 30.42 days instead of calendar days, used for prorating salary and leave deductions [Tickets: TSSD-4368]
- **Calendar Days**: The actual number of days in a specific month, which can vary (e.g., 28, 29, 30, or 31 days depending on the month and leap year status). [Tickets: TSSD-1301]
- **Global Daily Rate Configuration**: A system-wide setting that determines how daily wage deductions are calculated, which takes precedence over individual policy configurations [Tickets: TSSD-1753]
