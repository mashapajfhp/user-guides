## Glossary

- **Working Days**: A standard of 26 working days per month, calculated as 365 (annual days) - 52 (weekly offs) = 313 total working days, divided by 12 months to yield 26.08 working days per month [Tickets: TSSD-4905, TSSD-3648, TSSD-4292, TSSD-4464, TSSD-1753, TSSD-1807]
- **Daily Wage Calculator**: Calculated by dividing monthly basic salary by a fixed number of days (platform uses 30 days), used in computing end-of-service benefits [Tickets: TSSD-2648, TSSD-2043, TSSD-4464]
- **Unpaid Leave Deduction**: Calculation of salary reduction based on number of days an employee is absent without pay, using daily wage rate divided by actual calendar days in the month. [Tickets: TSSD-2648, TSSD-1581, TSSD-2532, TSSD-1301, TSSD-1807]
- **Daily Pay Calculation**: Calculated by dividing total monthly salary by 30.41 to determine daily wage rate [Tickets: TSSD-1581]
- **End of Service (EOS) Gratuity**: A financial benefit calculated based on an employee's total service period, typically computed using daily wage and years of service, with specific calculation rules for different service duration brackets. [Tickets: TSSD-4179, TSSD-2605, TSSD-4742, TSSD-1625, TSSD-2252]
- **Leap Year**: A year containing 366 days instead of the standard 365 days, occurring every 4 years (with some exceptions), which requires special handling in date-based calculations. [Tickets: TSSD-4179]
- **Pro-Rated Amount**: A proportional salary calculation for partial months, typically computed by dividing total salary by 30 and multiplying by days worked [Tickets: TSSD-2605]
- **Monthly Accrual Policy**: A leave policy where leave days are earned and granted at the end of each month, not prorated for incomplete months. [Tickets: TSSD-4742]
- **Conditional Accrual Policy**: A leave policy where leave days are earned daily, allowing for prorated accrual across partial months. [Tickets: TSSD-4742]
- **Service Duration**: Total number of days worked, calculated by dividing total days by 365, including considerations for leap years [Tickets: TSSD-1625]
- **Gratuity Calculation**: Method of calculating employee's terminal benefits using daily wage, service duration, and different rates for first 5 years (21 days per year) and subsequent years (30 days per year) [Tickets: TSSD-2252, TSSD-2043]
- **Daily Rate Divisor**: The number of days used to calculate the daily wage rate in leave salary calculations, which can be configured in the system's leave policy [Tickets: TSSD-4731]
- **Leave Salary Accrual**: The cumulative calculation of vacation leave monetary value, which can vary based on the number of working days in a given month [Tickets: TSSD-3648]
- **Daily Wage Proration**: Calculation of salary or leave compensation based on a daily rate, typically derived by dividing monthly salary by a specific number of days (e.g., 30 or 30.42). [Tickets: TSSD-2321, TSSD-4292]
- **Salary Divisor**: The number of days used to calculate the daily wage rate, which can vary between 30 and 30.42 days depending on the specific configuration. [Tickets: TSSD-2321]
- **Prorated Salary**: A partial salary calculation based on the exact number of days worked, typically used when an employee starts or leaves mid-month [Tickets: TSSD-3058]
- **Daily Wage Divisor**: A fixed value of 30.42 used to calculate the daily wage by dividing monthly salary, instead of using calendar days [Tickets: TSSD-4368]
- **Global Daily Rate Configuration**: A system-wide setting that determines how daily wage calculations are performed for unpaid leave, which takes precedence over individual policy configurations. [Tickets: TSSD-1753]
