## Glossary

- **Working Days**: Standard calculation of 26 working days per month, derived from 365 annual days minus 52 weekly offs, resulting in 313 working days per year divided by 12 months [Tickets: TSSD-4905, TSSD-3648, TSSD-4292, TSSD-4464, TSSD-1807]
- **Daily Wage Calculator**: A system configuration that determines how daily wage is calculated, typically by dividing salary components by the number of working days. In this system, the payroll settings version takes precedence over leave policy configurations. [Tickets: TSSD-2648, TSSD-4179, TSSD-2252, TSSD-2043, TSSD-4464]
- **Unpaid Leave Deduction**: Leave without pay, where daily wage deductions are calculated based on the daily wage calculator configured in payroll settings. [Tickets: TSSD-2648, TSSD-4368, TSSD-1301]
- **Daily Pay Calculation**: Calculating daily wage by dividing total monthly salary by 30.41 (average days in a month) [Tickets: TSSD-1581]
- **End of Service (EOS) Gratuity**: A benefit calculation for employees leaving a company, typically based on years of service and daily wage, calculated using a specific formula (21 days per year for service between 1-5 years). [Tickets: TSSD-4179, TSSD-2605, TSSD-4742, TSSD-1625, TSSD-2252]
- **Pro-rated Amount**: A proportional calculation of salary or benefits based on the actual days worked in a partial period, typically calculated by dividing the total amount by 30 days [Tickets: TSSD-2605]
- **Monthly Accrual Policy**: Leave days are earned and accrued at the end of each month, not prorated for incomplete months. [Tickets: TSSD-4742]
- **Conditional Accrual Policy**: Leave days are earned daily instead of being accumulated at month-end, providing more flexible accrual tracking. [Tickets: TSSD-4742]
- **Service Days**: Total number of days worked, calculated by counting from start date to departure date, including special considerations like leap year days [Tickets: TSSD-1625]
- **Gratuity Calculation**: The process of computing end-of-service benefits for an employee based on their length of service, basic salary, and applicable calculation method. [Tickets: TSSD-2043]
- **Daily Rate Configuration**: Policy setting that determines how daily wage is calculated, including the number of days used to divide monthly salary [Tickets: TSSD-4731, TSSD-1807]
- **Leave Salary Accrual**: Cumulative calculation of vacation leave monetary value, which can vary based on monthly working days configuration [Tickets: TSSD-3648]
- **Daily Wage Calculation**: Method of calculating daily rate by dividing total monthly salary (basic + allowances) by a specific number of days (typically 30 or 30.42) [Tickets: TSSD-2321]
- **Salary Proration**: Calculating a proportional salary amount based on actual working days in a month [Tickets: TSSD-2321]
- **Prorated Salary**: Partial salary calculation based on the exact number of days worked, typically calculated by dividing monthly salary by total calendar days and multiplying by days actually worked [Tickets: TSSD-3058]
- **Daily Wage Divisor**: A system-configured value (30.42 days) used to calculate the daily rate of an employee's salary for unpaid leave deductions [Tickets: TSSD-4368]
- **Unpaid Leave Daily Rate**: The method of calculating salary deduction for days without pay, typically calculated by dividing basic salary by total working days [Tickets: TSSD-1753]
